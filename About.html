About ExchangeME

ExchangeME was created to address the challenges of finding accurate, real-time currency exchange rates. Whether you're a business professional managing international transactions or a traveler planning your next trip, we designed ExchangeME to give you quick, reliable, and comprehensive currency information to make informed financial decisions.

The Problem We’re Solving

Travelers and business professionals often struggle to find accurate and up-to-date exchange rates. This can lead to overpaying for currency conversion or making uninformed decisions that affect budgets and financial goals. Our goal is to solve this issue by offering a reliable, easy-to-use platform that gives users access to real-time exchange rates, historical data, and customized alerts for favorable currency rates.

Our Mission

At ExchangeME, our mission is to help users access the best exchange rates and empower them to make better financial decisions. By providing accurate and real-time data, along with tools like historical charts and customizable alerts, we aim to help people save money and plan effectively for both short-term and long-term financial goals.

Project Overview

The ExchangeME platform is built with simplicity and efficiency in mind. Through a sleek and user-friendly interface, users can easily convert currencies, track historical trends, and set up alerts to receive notifications when exchange rates hit desirable levels. The platform relies on the Fixer.io API for real-time currency data.

Features of the ExchangeME Platform

  • Real-Time Exchange Rates: Get accurate, up-to-date exchange rates sourced from trusted financial data providers.
  • Currency Converter: Convert any amount between selected currencies with ease and accuracy.
  • Historical Data: Track and analyze currency performance over time with interactive, easy-to-read charts.
  • Alert System: Set customized alerts for specific exchange rate thresholds and receive notifications when rates meet your criteria.

Our Target Audience

ExchangeME is designed to serve a wide range of users who deal with currency exchanges:

  • Travelers: Easily manage currency conversions for international trips and avoid overpaying at exchange kiosks.
  • Business Professionals: Stay on top of currency fluctuations to ensure you’re making the most informed financial decisions in global transactions.
  • Anyone Dealing with Foreign Currency: Get access to reliable rates for personal or business transactions and make more informed choices when converting money.

Technological Stack

Our platform uses modern technologies to ensure optimal performance and a smooth user experience:

  • Fixer.io API: Provides real-time currency data and conversion rates from reliable sources.
  • SCSS: Makes it easier to style the application efficiently, allowing for better maintainability and scalability of the code.
  • Supabase: A powerful backend-as-a-service that stores user preferences and historical rate data.
  • Node.js: Powers our backend server, handling all requests, processing data, and communicating with external APIs.

Future Plans

We’re excited to expand the ExchangeME platform with additional features, including:

  • Support for more currencies and countries
  • Enhanced alert system with greater customization options
  • User account management for better tracking of personal preferences and historical data
